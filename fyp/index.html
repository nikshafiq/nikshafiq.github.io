Nik Mohamad Shafiq bin Mohd Nazri [18951]

Problem Statement

Currently most of the motorcycle technology is still far behind other vehicle especially when come to data displays on the dashboard of the motorcycle.

There are also need for GPS system in the display which can significantly ease the rider on how they could they reach their destination without being lost just like we have in cars.

Abstract

“Smart Motorcycle Display System” is a system that provide the convenient, cool looking displays of the information of the motorcycle. The display projects GPS system on the main screen as the main purpose of the display. Plus, at the corner of the display, all of other indicators such as speedometer, RPM meter, fuel indicator, gear position indicator and signal also will be included. This will give the rider all the info needed to enjoy their ride.
